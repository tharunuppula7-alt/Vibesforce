🏥 OncoAssist – AI Patient Scheduling Agent
📌 Overview

OncoAssist is an AI-powered Patient Scheduling Agent built on Salesforce to transform patient interactions in oncology care. It acts as a Digital Front Door for Onco Global, enabling patients to access healthcare services quickly without relying on overloaded contact centers.

With 450+ daily calls handled by just 7 agents, Onco Global faces delays, inefficiencies, and missed appointments. OncoAssist addresses these challenges using AI-driven automation and self-service capabilities.

🎯 Problem Statement
⏳ Long patient wait times due to high call volume
🏥 Difficulty managing appointments across multiple hospitals
❌ High no-show rates due to lack of reminders and engagement
💡 Solution

OncoAssist introduces a self-service AI agent that enables patients to:

📅 Book, modify, and cancel appointments
👨‍⚕️ Discover doctors and departments
🏥 Access hospital and treatment information
🤖 Receive automated support without human intervention
⚙️ Key Features
🤖 AI Patient Agent (Agentforce)
Conversational interface for patient queries
Natural Language Understanding (NLU)
Executes actions like booking and cancellations
🌐 Web-Based Self-Service Portal
Built using Experience Cloud
Available 24/7 for patient access
🏥 Specialty & Location Discovery
Information about oncology services
Multi-location hospital discovery
👨‍⚕️ Doctor & Department Search
Search doctors by specialty or location
Easy department browsing
📅 Appointment Management
Book appointments
Modify existing bookings
Cancel appointments
🔄 Flexible Booking
Book with a specific doctor
Or book under a department
⚠️ No-Show Reduction
Automated reminders
Improved hospital efficiency and utilization

🧪 How It Works
Patient opens the web portal
Interacts with the AI agent
Searches for doctor or department
Books or manages an appointment
Receives automated confirmations and reminders
🚀 Impact
⏱️ Reduced patient wait times
